About Me

My first car was a 1963 Ford Falcon Sprint with a broken speedometer and a working tachometer. Armed with a few mathematical formulas and an Apple IIe, I wrote a program in basic to calculate speed using the RPM displayed on the tachometer. I fell in love with programming that day. I held a variety of jobs over the years, but programming was always something more for me. I learned several languages in college including Basic, Pascal, C, and C++. Even though I took a different career path, I wrote a few applications during the early days.
